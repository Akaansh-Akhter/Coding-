The Falcon

The Falcon is UAE's official national bird. The Falcon is the national bird of the UAE. It is only fitting that this majestic bird stands as one of UAE’s national symbols, as falconry itself is a major aspect of Emirati culture and traditions. To the UAE, the falcon represents strength, national heritage and agility. The image of the falcon is seen on the UAE emblem as well as most of the bank notes.

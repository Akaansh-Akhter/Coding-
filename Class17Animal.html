The Arabian Oryx

Arabian Oryx is the United Arab Emirates’ official national animal. It is the largest mammal that lives in the deserts of Arab regions. This animal is a desert antelope well suited to its rugged wilderness. Her glossy white coat represents the sun’s light, and her hooves look like a paddle and stretch out, which allows the animal a wide field while walking on the sandy dirt. These animals are renowned for their endurance and grace and inspire various Arab poets.

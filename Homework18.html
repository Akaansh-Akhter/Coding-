    • Central Park is an urban park in New York City located between the Upper West and Upper East Sides of Manhattan. It is the fifth-largest park in the city by area, covering 843 acres (341 ha). It is the most visited urban park in the United States, with an estimated 42 million visitors annually as of 2016. It is extremely popular due to its serene grace which attracts tourists to come and visit it.The park is home to plenty of wildlife that depend on it for survival.

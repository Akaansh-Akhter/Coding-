Tom and Jerry

A summary of Tom and Jerry:

Two main characters are a cat and mouse. The cat is chasing the mouse in an endless run with hilarious failures while the mouse always gets away in quick witted plans that cause watchers to feel as though they are apart of the chase and the run between these childhood cartoon characters.

Produced by

Tom and Jerry is an American animated media franchise and series of comedy short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera and produced by the Warner Animation Group and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures..

Characters from the Tom and Jerry series.

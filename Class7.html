Facts

Dubai Scholars is a school of prestige and has a very strict discipline policy. All the students that attend are well-behaved as compared to other schools. This school is a British School located in Al Quasis, Dubai, UAE. It is also one of the oldest schools in the UAE. It was founded in 1976 and has been very successful.

The school has an emblem that is extremely memorable and is on all students uniforms as seen below. Dubai Scholars is also a high standard school and has been rated good by the KHDA over a course of 10 years. The school provides transport to all of Dubai and Sharjah.

There are many activities that take pace in my school from studying in the classrooms to experiments in the science labs. Some others are: playing sports on the football field, basketball court or the swimming pool.

My personal favourite part of the school is the library where we (students are allowed to pick up any one book from the hundreds that are there.


There are many subjects that we learn, they include:

  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Physics
  • English
  • Math
    • and so much more

FA's are taken after each lesson and contribute a high amount in our report cards

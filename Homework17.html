How old is chess?

To be frank, no one knows for sure how old the game is. Most people estimate that we invented this version of chess in the 6th century AD, and we are in the 21st century. Do some math, one century = 100 years, 21 minus 6 is 14. So we may have been playing this game for 1400 staggering years! That is mind-blowing, especially considering it has stood the test of time! The earliest version of chess was called chaturanga, which translates “of the military”.

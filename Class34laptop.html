What is a Laptop? Alternatively referred to as a notebook, a laptop is a portable computer that is more efficient, and nearly as powerful, as a desktop computer.A laptop or note book facilitates learning in many ways: 1.The laptop along with references and notes is with you always and the internet libraries and other website have tools that help students' format assignments and references in MLA or APA formats. 2.With a laptop a student can take notes in class, type up assignments and much more.
